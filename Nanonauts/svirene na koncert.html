shh SUUIIIII

We are the Nanonauts.

Our names are maxim boqn krasimir i kaloqn a i elon musk e tuka,.

koko – Singer extraordinaire

kaloqn е певицата на grupata boyanovci. Обича да пее постоянно – не само когато си играе с нанонаутите! Майка му и баща му казват, че се е налущал да пее, преди да се научи да говори! kaloqn също играе кларинет и се учи на алто саксофона.

If you hit a wrong note or make a mistake, don’t stop playing – just keep going and look as though you meant it!

koko also plays clarinet and is learning the alto saxophone.

krasio – One-man rhythm section

красио имаше музика в кръвта си от деня, в който се роди. И майка му, и баща му играят инструменти и са му взели първия комплект барабани, когато е бил само на пет години, което е довело до неприятности със съседите. На някои песни свири на бас китара и на някои песни барабаните. krasio обича да играе в Нанонаутите, но мрази да носи барабанния си комплект наоколо.

boyan – If it's got strings, she'll play it

Acoustic guitar? Electric lead guitar? Ukelele? Harp?boyan can play them all. She started off by making homemade guitars from cardboard boxes and elastic bands till her uncle took pity on her and bought her a Spanish guitar for Christmas. After a few lessons at school she was flying!

maxim – Keyboard maestro

maxim has fourth grade piano but in secret prefers to play electronic keyboards. She loves synthesizer sounds and arguing with Holly over who should play the basslines.

Nano Noodling – kupete diska!

Great if you're learning to play!

Are your kids learning to play musical instruments? Then why not order this great CD of the Nanonauts' favourite songs! As well as having all the complete songs, the CD includes versions of the songs with each instrument missing – so you can play along with your own instrument. Not only that, but the solo parts and the music are also included so you can copy what to play.
